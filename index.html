The Importance of Semantic HTML for SEO

Semantic HTML5 elements like <header>, <nav>, <main>, <article>, and <footer> help search engines (and accessibility tools) understand the structure and hierarchy of your content. This structural clarity is a key factor in technical SEO.

Leveraging Headings for Content Organization

Proper use of headings (H1, H2, H3, etc.) creates a content outline. The H1 is the main topic, and H2s are the primary subtopics. Ensure your target keywords naturally appear in these headings.

Tips for Writing a Great H1 Tag

Your H1 should be compelling, accurate, and include your primary keyword. Remember, it should match the main topic of the page.
